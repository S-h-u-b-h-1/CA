# Legislative & Regulatory Source Connectors

The research workspace matches and cites legislative authorities from a structured registry populated with standard legal and accounting materials.

## Supported Sources

- **Income Tax Act:** Statutory tax provisions (e.g. Sections 143(1), 194A, 154, 139(1)).
- **Income Tax Rules:** Detailed direct tax administration rules.
- **CBDT Circulars:** Direct tax guidelines issued by the central board (e.g. Circular 21/2024).
- **CGST Rules:** Rules governing indirect tax execution (e.g. Rule 36(4)).
- **Companies Act:** Corporate governance rules (e.g. Section 135 CSR).
- **ICAI Guidance Notes:** Accounting audit guidelines and checklists.
