# Client Tax Intelligence Module

The **Client Tax Intelligence Module** forms the key component of the Phase 6 analytics engine. It automatically merges information parsed from diverse taxpayer documents (e.g., Form 26AS, Annual Information Statements) into a consolidated record.

## Consolidations & Profiles
- **ClientTaxProfile:** Maps PAN, client identity, Assessment Year, and processing state.
- **TaxSummary:** Caches aggregate totals including TDS credit, Interest, Dividend, Salary, SFT transactions, Mutual Funds, Immovable Property, and active tax refund/demand amounts.
- **ClientTaxInsight:** Caches discrepancies and high-value notifications generated by the Rules Engine.
